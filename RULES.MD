## Blackjack

### Overview

Blackjack is a classic card game where the objective is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over (busting).

### Card Values

- **Number cards (2-10)**: Face value
- **Face cards (Jack, Queen, King)**: Worth 10 points
- **Ace**: Worth 11 points (or 1 point if counting it as 11 would cause a bust)

### Gameplay

1. **Deal**: Both the player and dealer receive 2 cards each
2. **Player's Turn**:
   - The player sees both their cards and one of the dealer's cards (the dealer's hole card is hidden)
   - The player can choose to:
     - **Hit**: Take another card to try to get closer to 21
     - **Stand**: Keep the current hand and pass the turn to the dealer
   - The player continues taking turns until they decide to stand or exceed 21 (bust)
3. **Dealer's Turn**:
   - The dealer reveals their hidden hole card
   - The dealer must hit if their hand value is less than 17
   - The dealer must stand if their hand value is 17 or higher
4. **Determine Winner**:
   - If the player busts (exceeds 21): **Dealer wins**
   - If the dealer busts and the player didn't: **Player wins**
   - If neither busts: The hand with the highest value wins
   - If both hands have the same value: **Push** (a tie - no winner)

### Winning Conditions

- **Player Win**: Player's score is higher than the dealer's without busting
- **Dealer Win**: Dealer's score is higher than the player's, or the player busts
- **Push**: Both player and dealer have the same score

## Poker

### Overview

Poker is a strategic card game where players bet based on the strength of their hands and attempt to win chips from other players. The game uses Texas Hold'em rules.

### Hand Rankings (Lowest to Highest)

1. **High Card**: No matching cards; determined by the highest card
2. **One Pair**: Two cards of the same rank
3. **Two Pair**: Two different pairs
4. **Three of a Kind**: Three cards of the same rank
5. **Straight**: Five cards in sequential rank (e.g., 5-6-7-8-9)
6. **Flush**: Five cards of the same suit
7. **Full House**: Three of a kind plus a pair
8. **Four of a Kind**: Four cards of the same rank
9. **Straight Flush**: Five cards in sequence and of the same suit
10. **Royal Flush**: Ace-King-Queen-Jack-Ten all of the same suit (highest hand)

### Gameplay Phases

1. **Pre-Flop**:
   - Each player receives 2 private cards (hole cards)
   - Players place bets or fold based on their hand strength
2. **Flop**:
   - Three community cards are revealed face-up on the table
   - Players place additional bets or fold

3. **Turn**:
   - A fourth community card is revealed
   - Players place additional bets or fold

4. **River**:
   - A fifth and final community card is revealed
   - Players place final bets or fold

5. **Showdown**:
   - Remaining players reveal their cards
   - Each player makes the best 5-card hand using their 2 hole cards and the 5 community cards
   - The player with the highest-ranked hand wins the pot

### Betting Actions

- **Fold**: Discard your hand and exit the round
- **Check**: Stay in the hand without betting (if no bet is made)
- **Bet**: Place chips in the pot
- **Call**: Match the current bet amount
- **Raise**: Increase the current bet amount

## Slot Machine

### Overview

Slot Machine is a game of pure chance where the player spins three reels and wins payouts based on matching symbols.

### Symbols

The three reels can display any of the following symbols:

- 🍒 Cherry
- 🍋 Lemon
- 🔔 Bell
- 💎 Diamond
- ⭐ Star
- 7️⃣ Seven
- ➖ Bar

### Payouts

Winnings are determined by the combination of symbols on all three reels and the amount wagered. All matching symbols must appear on the same spin.

| Combination             | Payout   |
| ----------------------- | -------- |
| 7️⃣ 7️⃣ 7️⃣ Three Sevens   | Bet × 20 |
| ➖ ➖ ➖ Three Bars     | Bet × 12 |
| 🔔 🔔 🔔 Three Bells    | Bet × 10 |
| ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ Three Stars    | Bet × 8  |
| 🍒 🍒 🍒 Three Cherries | Bet × 6  |
| 🍒 🍒 Two Cherries      | Bet × 2  |
| 🍒 One Cherry           | Bet × 1  |
| No matches              | 0 (loss) |

### Gameplay

1. **Select Bet**: Choose the amount to wager on the spin
2. **Spin**: Initiate the spin - each reel randomly displays a symbol
3. **Check Results**: The machine calculates if you've matched any symbols
4. **Receive Payout**: If you have a winning combination, the payout is added to your credits
5. **Continue**: Choose to spin again or exit the game

### Strategy

- Slot machines are entirely based on luck - each spin is independent
- The house edge is built into the payout structure
- Set a budget and stick to it for responsible gaming
